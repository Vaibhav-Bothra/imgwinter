Why use Quizzer?



Quizzer is a free student-response tool for administering quizzes, facilitating discussions, and collecting survey data and is trusted by many who are part of the educational sector.


It is a game-based classroom response system played in real time.It trains kids for real timed competition which help them to get better at quick thinking and time management.


Quizzer also provides facilities like making custom quizes by either adding your own questions or by using thousands of question that are made with the help of the community.Quizzer has real time leaderboards so as to enable students to know where they are at a certain time in a quiz.


quizzer has something for both the students and the teachers.


Students can practise questions from a specific topic on their own as well.
